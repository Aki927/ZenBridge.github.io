Yoga

Beginner

Vinyasa Yoga focuses on linking yoga poses, or "asanas", together in a flowing way. There are both physical and mental benefits. It is relatively more fast-paced than other types of yoga, making it exceptional for those who want to increase cardiovascular health and build endurance. (video is a demo only)

Intermediate

Yin Yoga consists of long-held, poses that typically work your lower body. Some poses are held up to five minutes. Yin is almost entirely passive, although some Yin asanas contain Yang elements. During the asanas, muscles are relaxed to avoid muscle spasm, which can be beneficial as it engages your muscles for longer intervals. (video is a demo only)

Advanced

Hatha Yoga usually has physical poses and breathing techniques combined which are executed more slowly and with more static holds than a Vinyasa. Hatha means ‘force’ hence it is defined as ‘the yoga of force. This type of yoga enables us to connect with ourselves physically, mentally, emotionally and spiritually. (video is a demo only)
